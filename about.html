The Last Flicker is a supernatural thriller that follows three friends—Josh, Amilia, and Dav—as they confront a demonic spirit trapped in a haunted church. Their curiosity leads them into a harrowing experience that tests their courage, friendship, and fate.

This project was developed as part of our coursework for IAT 313 D100, focusing on narrative development and multimedia integration.

Our Creative Journey

Our journey began with a shared fascination for supernatural tales and the question: What happens when curiosity leads us into the unknown? Under Michelle's leadership, we combined our diverse skills to bring The Last Flicker to life.

  • Michelle crafted a compelling narrative and developed multifaceted characters.
  • Kristin and Kaleigh visualized the story through detailed illustrations and storyboards, establishing the haunting aesthetic.
  • AJ worked on the technical development, integrating animations and interactive elements. AJ's expertise in coding and animating was crucial in implementing the dynamic features that enhance user engagement.
  • Michelle coordinated schedules and facilitated communication, keeping the project on track.

Collaboratively, we navigated the challenges of merging storytelling with interactive media, striving to create an experience that is both thrilling and meaningful.

Technologies & Designs

We utilized a range of tools and technologies to realize our vision:

  • Development: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Animations: GreenSock Animation Platform (GSAP)
  • Design Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate
  • Sound Design: Logic Pro X, Audacity
  • Version Control: GitHub

Our design philosophy centered on immersion. Through the combination of evocative illustrations, atmospheric sound design, and interactive web elements, we aimed to transport users into the world of The Last Flicker. The web development and animations was key to integrating these elements seamlessly.
